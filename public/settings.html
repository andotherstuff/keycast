Understanding Nostr Keys

üìö What is a Private Key?

Your private key (also called nsec or secret key) is like a master password for your Nostr identity. It's a cryptographic key that proves you own your account.

Key Concepts:

  • Public Key (npub): Your identity - safe to share with everyone
  • Private Key (nsec): Your password - NEVER share this
  • No Password Reset: If you lose your private key, your account is gone forever
  • No Central Authority: Nobody (not even Keycast) can recover your key

‚ö†Ô∏è Before You Continue

Make sure you understand:

  • Your private key cannot be changed or reset
  • Anyone with your private key has full control of your identity
  • You are solely responsible for keeping it secure
